Machine Learning

FishСatch

Fishcatch is a startup with a handy app that allows anglers to find out what fish they can catch on any given day, anywhere in the world. Our Bellumaco team conducted research and combined their knowledge and efforts with Fishcatch engineers to create and implement such a service.

Challenge

Through our partnership, a huge amount of data has been collected on 2.5 million catches, and each data point has been annotated with the fish species, location, date, and time. We also took care of detailed weather information, which included: precipitation, wind speed, pressure, and air temperature at that particular catch location. From this, our team created a global climate database that contained information on historical weather conditions, vegetation, and geology. With all of the data our team scrupulously collected, we created a model that can predict the likelihood of catching different kinds of fish around the world.

Results:

The development and implementation were done in Python. We used cloud-based managed services for efficient setup and training, as well as model deployment. Catch data was collected from a variety of internal and external sources. Additionally, we developed data pipelines to handle data from internal and external APIs.

Work done:

Our team took a comprehensive approach to the tasks and accepted this interesting challenge. Team and professional work, powerful machine learning, and engagement brought results.
