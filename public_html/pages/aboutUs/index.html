About us

Company history

Bellumaco was founded in 2020. At that time our team consisted of 15 people united by the goal to create a service where customers understand and solve their problems, and not just make money. And as time has passed the team has grown, the number of projects and professionalism has increased. But one thing has not changed - the approach.

Company benefits

We prioritize quality in the long term over profitability in the short term. We are not constrained by standard patterns, so we have a huge layer of possibilities for implementation. Our team uses innovative and reliable development methods. We create more than just a system, we create a product.
